Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your Waco account and associated data, please follow one of the options below:

  1. Send an email to support@waco.com with the subject line “Delete My Account”.
  2. Include your registered email address used for Facebook or Google login.
  3. We will verify your request and permanently delete your data from our servers within 7 business days.

If you logged in via Facebook, you can also remove our app permissions directly from your Facebook settings.
